Game Rules

Welcome to **Color Clash**! Your goal is to match at least 3 tiles of the same color vertically or horizontally to score points and earn coins. Here’s how you play:

🎮 How to Play:

  • Click or tap on a tile to select it.
  • Swap two adjacent tiles to create a match of three or more tiles.
  • Special tiles (gold or green) give bonus points and effects!

💡 Special Tiles:

  • 💛 Gold Tile: Awards extra **50 points** and **1 coin**.
  • 💚 Green Tile: Grants **EXP** – earn enough to level up!

🛒 Store:

Use your coins and points to purchase upgrades in the store. Upgrades include:

  • 🔹 Power-Ups – Destroy nearby tiles!
  • 🔹 Extra Moves – Gain additional turns!
  • 🔹 Bonus Multipliers – Increase points gained!

🏆 Achievements:

Complete in-game missions to unlock achievements and bonus rewards!
